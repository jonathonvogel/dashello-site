Run Your Business Like An Airplane

Fly Far and Fast

Think of your business like an airplane. You're the pilot. Leadership is your cockpit. And Dashello is your instrument panel — giving you real-time optics on every part of your operation so you can course-correct before things go wrong. Because if you can't see how your airplane is doing at a glance, you can't make the decisions that keep it in the air.

Integrating the proven principles of Business Made Simple by Donald Miller, here are the six key areas your dashboard needs to track:

Leadership = Cockpit This is where direction is set. Leadership charts the course, makes the strategic calls, and keeps everyone aligned. Without a steady hand in the cockpit, the plane has no destination.
Cash Flow = Fuel Without it, you're not going anywhere. You need to know your fuel level at all times — how much is coming in, how much is going out, and whether you have enough to reach your destination.
Products = Wings Your products are what lift your business off the ground. If they're not generating lift — if they're not solving a real problem — your plane stays on the tarmac. Track which ones are flying and which ones need work.
Sales = Left Engine Your left engine generates thrust by closing deals and bringing in revenue. You need to see which channels are performing, what's converting, and where the momentum is coming from.
Marketing = Right Engine Your right engine builds awareness, generates leads, and keeps the pipeline full. Both engines need to fire together or you fly in circles. Track campaign performance, lead flow, and cost per acquisition.
Overhead = Body The fuselage carries everything — your team, your tools, your operating costs. Keep it lean and your plane is agile. Let it get too heavy and you'll struggle to get off the ground.

When you can see every gauge at a glance — direction, fuel, lift, thrust, and weight — you can make better and faster decisions. You get off the runway and fly far and fast. That's what Dashello gives you.
