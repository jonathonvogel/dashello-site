Our Values

What we look for in every team member

Three values that define how we work, how we grow, and how we show up for each other.

Smart Working

Do you ruthlessly pursue the most effective ways to get things done, improve processes, save time, and save money? We're always looking for a better way.

Unconditional Love

Do you seek to serve and give of yourself for the good of others? Can you have tough conversations and be transparent and upfront with expectations?

Consciously Humble

Do you trust your team enough to share problems and defeats? Do you invite constructive feedback to improve, and win and lose as one team?

Habits We Strive To Keep

How we stay aligned, focused, and growing

Simple practices that keep our team moving in the same direction.

Habit 01

Weekly all-staff & priority check

We over-communicate with each other and ensure the most important projects are always moving forward. We filter every decision by our mission so we get the right work done.

Habit 02

We double-check our work

We're all on a journey of continuous improvement. By reviewing our work, we invite healthy feedback so we can create better products and strengthen our skills.

Habit 03

We spread the word

Every business owner deserves to leave for home at the end of the day knowing their business is healthy and on track. We share that mission with the world.

Our Mission

Every business owner deserves to know their business is healthy

But in order to succeed, business owners need help. We help by building a business dashboard software that simplifies their data in a way that is quick to read and understand so they can make the best and fastest decision and succeed in growing their business.

If you are smart working, passionately enthusiastic, and consciously humble, join our mission.
